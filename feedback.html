Wing Check Feedback

Help tune the Gurnard wind ratings

What to send

The app gets its wind forecast from Open-Meteo and live wind from Bramblemet at Bramble Bank. The bit that needs local help is the rating: how the wind actually felt for wing foiling at Gurnard versus what the app scored it.

Send the date, time, what the app scored that hour, and what you'd have scored it. A short note helps a lot.

Send it back to me in a message. If you have this app, you probably already know where to find me.

Rating guide

GreenGood wind, cross-shore or better - get out there.
AmberRideable but marginal - light, gusty, or a bit onshore.
RedSkip it - too light, too strong, or offshore.
